About This App

NT Greek Vocab Trainer helps you learn and review common New Testament Greek vocabulary by chapter according to Bill Mounce's "Basics of biblical Greek".

Use Learn to study flashcards, Test to quiz yourself, and Progress to see your recent scores and most missed words.

Focus Mode tests the words you struggle with most, helping you review weaker vocabulary more intentionally.

Why Learn These Words?

This app includes over 300 common New Testament Greek words. Together, these words appear so often that they make up a large portion of the Greek New Testament. (Over 80%)

Even before learning every grammar rule, recognizing common Greek words can help you read more carefully, notice repeated ideas, and better understand how biblical passages are being communicated.

The goal is not to replace serious Greek study, but to give you a strong vocabulary foundation so the New Testament feels less unfamiliar as you continue learning.
